   7    Background:
   8      Given the following "users" exist:
   9        | username | firstname | lastname | email                | interests      |

  11        | user1    | User      | 1        | user1@example.com    | Cat,Dog,Turtle |
  12      And the following "system role assigns" exist:
  13        | user     | course               | role      |

  16        | name         | isstandard |
  17        | Neverusedtag | 1          |
  18  
  19    Scenario: Deleting a tag with javascript disabled
  20      When I log in as "manager1"
  21      And I navigate to "Manage tags" node in "Site administration > Appearance"
  22      And I follow "Default collection"

  24      And I should see "Tag(s) deleted"

  26      And I navigate to "Participants" node in "Site pages"
  27      And I follow "User 1"
